"Don’t Take That Tone With Me Young Man?"

We Nurture Self-Esteem, Social Skills And Discipline For Life.

We provide a world-acclaimed music and performing arts curriculum that educates, motivates and builds character in boys through unparalleled opportunities for performance, leadership, travel and community service.

We’re Not All About Vibrating Tonsils And Belting Out Chords...

We Read Music, Understand Notes And Get A Complete Musical Education.

A key component of our overall music education curriculum, our Music Reading Program incorporates a professional staff to teach the sight-reading, writing and comprehension of the language of music. The course is taught by four credentialed instructors. Classes take place Saturdays from September to June.
